How to take a good quality photo ...

Time needed: 3-5 minutes
Awesome rating: 10% awesome

Are you intrested in being a photographer or simply knowing how to take good photos? Starting off with the camera on your phone is quite simple to learn! Follow these steps and you'll learn how to take a good, quality photos as a beginner!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Your phone
  • Some type of camera app
  • Something or someone to photograph

  1. Make sure to have a clean camera lense!

    This is necessary because smudges and smears on your camera lens will make your photos blurrier.

  2. Set up your camera grid.

    Aligning the subject of your photo with the lines on the grid makes for better composition in photography. For this step you need to open the settings app on your phone, find the camera app section, scroll down to composition and make sure "Grid" is on.

  3. Decided what camera you want to use and if you need flash or not.

    Depending on what you are taking a picture of decide what camera would be best to use. The front-facing camera is good for taking selfies. The rear-facing camera is better fortaking pictures of other things and things at a distance. If you are taking pictures in a dark environment, you may want to enable the flash.Tap the icon that resembles a lightning bolt at the top of the screen to enable or disable the flash. Although do keep in mind the flash only works on the rear camera if you are using the app your phone comes with. So if you plan on taking a selfie make sure to find a different source of lighting!

  4. Zoom in and Set up timer (optional)

    You can generally zoom in by using the pinch-zoom gesture on your phone. To do so, place your thumb and index finger on the screen. Bring them together to zoom in, and spread them apart to zoom out. You can also tap 1x, 2x, 3x etc or use the slider bar at the bottom of the screen to zoom in and out.Zooming in can make pictures look grainy and pixelated. Generally, it’s best only to use a small amount of zoom or none at all. The timer is useful if you want to get in the picture before taking it. It will count down before taking a picture. To set a timer, tap the icon that resembles a clock timer and then select how many seconds you want it to count down. Generally, you can select 3 seconds, 5 seconds or 10 seconds.

  5. Focus the camera.

    Tap on the subject you would like to capture to focus the camera on it. If you want to focus on a specific area you can lock the focus point, long cick on the point you want to focus on. At the top of the camera view "AE/AF Lock" should pop up.

  6. Take the picture.

    To do so, tap the shutter button at the bottom-center of the screen. It resembles a large white circle. Make sure to take multiple photos. You often don’t get the best pictures on the first try. Take multiple pictures. Try taking pictures from multiple angles.
